# Welcome to GoTrackMoney Documentation

GoTrackMoney is a self-hosted, lightweight personal finance tracking application. It allows you to easily manage your expenses, incomes, categories, and keep track of your overall balance.

## Features

- **Intuitive Dashboard**: Overview of your total balance, recent expenses, and incomes.
- **Categorization**: Group your transactions by custom categories with emojis.
- **Periodic Transactions**: Automate recurring expenses or incomes.
- **Analytics**: Beautiful charts to visualize your spending habits.
- **Admin Panel**: Manage multiple users, reset passwords, and control access.
- **Multi-Language Support**: Available in English and Italian.

Explore the navigation menu to learn how to deploy the application, configure your server, and safely backup your financial data!
